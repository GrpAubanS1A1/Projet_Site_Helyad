Helyad through figures

Turnover

In the year 2019 - 2020, the turnover of HELYAD was 2.239.306 €. In the year 2020 - 2021 the turnover was 2.936.989 €, which represents an increase of 31%. However, this increase cannot be considered as a successful year, since the turnover of the year 2019 - 2020 was reached in 9 months, because Helyad was shortened. The turnover of 2019 - 2020 was reached in 3 months less than the year 2020 - 2021 which shows us that there was not an increase of 31 %, but on the contrary the turnover of helyad decreased by 1.7 % on the year 2020 - 2021.
