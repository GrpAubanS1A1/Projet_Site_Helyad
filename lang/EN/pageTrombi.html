The company is organized in a horizontal way, that is to say that there are the two managers: Nathalie Seguinot and Christophe Chusseau at the top, then everyone at the same level (even the alternating students). Everyone has as N+1 one of the two managers.

The organization of positions is equally simple:
each person has a job name, but all positions are encompassed in two categories:
 • technical consultants
 • functional consultants.

Functional consultants must find the simplest and most efficient way to meet the needs of clients to facilitate the work of technical consultants.

The technical consultants are there to develop the solutions requested by the customers. They are the ones who have the knowledge to program these solutions.
