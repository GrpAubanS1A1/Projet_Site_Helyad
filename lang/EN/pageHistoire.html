The story of HELYAD

1990

Company's birth under the name ANTEOR. It was later bought several times and took its current name: HELYAD.

1998

HELYAD links up with the software publisher INFOR to become an integrator and support partner, particularly for the INFORBaan LN software.

2020

The company is bought by MPP CONSEIL. The new head office is legally and officially located in Jard-sur-Mer.
