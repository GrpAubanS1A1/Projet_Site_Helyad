ERP

An ERP (Enterprise Resource System) software is a computer tool that allows the management of the company. Its particularity is to embed, in a single software and a single database, the functionalities necessary for the management of the whole activity of a company: accountant , commercial management , inventory...

The particularities of ERP :

  • UNIQUE
    ERP software must come from a single developer
  • RELIABLE
    It must be based on a real time update of the modified information in all the affected modules
  • UNICITY
    It must guarantee the user the uniqueness of information provided by the availability of the entire structure of the database from each of the modules, even taken individually
  • TRACEABILITY
    It must provide audit trails based on the guarantee of total traceability of management operations
  • COUVERTURE
    It must cover a management function (or sector), or the entire information system of the company
